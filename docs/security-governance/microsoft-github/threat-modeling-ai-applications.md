# Threat modeling AI applications | Microsoft Security Blog

> **Excerpt from the original:** We cannot anticipate every misuse or emergent behavior in AI systems. We can, however, identify what can go wrong, assess how bad it could be, and design systems that help reduce the likelihood or impact of those failure modes. That is the role of threat modeling: a structured way to identify, analyze, and prioritize risks early so teams can prepare for and limit the impact of real‑world failures or adversarial exploits.
