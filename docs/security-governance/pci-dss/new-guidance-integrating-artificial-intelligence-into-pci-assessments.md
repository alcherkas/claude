# New Guidance: Integrating Artificial Intelligence into PCI Assessments

Alicia Malone, 17 Mar 2025; "AI is a tool, not an assessor"

> **Excerpt from the original:** Artificial intelligence (AI) is transforming industries, and the PCI Security Standards Council (PCI SSC) has introduced new guidance to support the responsible use of AI in PCI assessments. The guidance provides a balance between leveraging the benefits of AI while maintaining the high standards of security that protect payment card data worldwide.
