# Lost in the Middle: How Language Models Use Long Contexts

Peer-reviewed TACL paper on how model performance can degrade when relevant information appears in the middle of a long context.

> **Excerpt from the original:** Language models often perform better when relevant information occurs at the beginning or end of their input context, and performance can substantially degrade when models must access relevant information in the middle of long contexts.
